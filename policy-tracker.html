Data note: This database is sourced from government notifications (PIB, MoEFCC, MNRE, BEE, PRS India), UNFCCC NDC submissions, and the India Climate & Environment Policy Landscape Report (April 2026). This MVP uses static data. A production version would automate updates via scheduled scraping of open-access government sources. Last updated: April 2026. Scope: This tracker covers India's compliance and statutory policy framework — the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme, BRSR, renewable energy policy, forest and biodiversity legislation, and international climate commitments. Voluntary carbon market project development (Verra VCS, Gold Standard, CDM legacy registries) is outside the scope of this version.
